# English Phonemes Not in Hebrew (American)

## Consonants
| Phoneme | Sound | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **θ** | unvoiced “th” | thin |
| **ð** | voiced “th” | this |
| **ŋ** | nasal “ng” | sung |
| **ɹ** | American R | red |
| **ɾ** | tap/flap | butter |

---

## Vowels

### Lax / Open Vowels
| Phoneme | Sound | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **æ** | short “a” | cat |
| **ɪ** | short “i” | sit |
| **ʊ** | short “u” | good |
| **ʌ** | central “uh” | sun |
| **ɔ** | open “o” | all |

### Central / Reduced Vowels
| Phoneme | Sound | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **ə** | schwa | about |
| **ᵊ** | weak schwa | pixel |
| **ɜ** | “er” vowel | her |
| **ᵻ** | between ə and ɪ | boxes |

### Additional Key Vowels
| Phoneme | Sound | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **ɑ** | “ah” | spa / father |
| **ɛ** | open-mid “e” | bed / hair |
